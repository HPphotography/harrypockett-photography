BlåBär
Nordic Living, in Putney.

A hospitality brand shoot for an independent London café and design space, BlåBär. The brief focused on showcasing the venue's morning ambience, pairing soft natural light with minimalist textures and candid moments to reflect the atmosphere, design, and everyday experience of the café.
