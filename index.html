About

Inspired by people
who shape the places around them

Originally from the South East of England, I'm a London-based photographer, working across documentary, portrait, and commercial photography in both digital and analogue formats.

I’m interested in how people inhabit the everyday–the gestures, routines, and in-between moments that quietly shape a place. My work is guided by observation rather than direction, whether I’m photographing a brand, a community, or an individual. I aim to create images that stay with you, rather than simply describe what’s in front of the camera.
