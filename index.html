About

Based in London.
At home anywhere.

I'm a documentary and commercial photographer working across London and internationally. My background is in street and travel photography — years spent shooting across India, Vietnam, Cambodia, Thailand, and further afield — and I bring that same instinct for authentic, unposed moments to every commercial project.

I shoot both digital and 35mm film, and I'm currently completing a professional photography course at the London Institute of Photography. Available for brand, restaurant, real estate, and editorial work.
